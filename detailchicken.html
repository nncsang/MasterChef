CHICKEN

CHICKEN

2 comment(s)

STEP 1


Put the chicken, wine, zest, bay leaf, thyme, garlic, onion and celery in a large saucepan. Add enough water to cover the ingredients. Bring to the boil then boil gently for 1 hour or until the chicken is very tender.

STEP 2


Remove from the heat and carefully strain through a colander into a large bowl. The liquid is the stock.

STEP 3


Once the chicken is cool enough to handle, remove the meat from the bones, discarding the skin and bones. Break the chicken meat up into small pieces.

STEP 4


Add the carrots to the stock in the saucepan then put over high heat and bring to the boil. Simmer for 15 minutes or until the carrots are soft. Add the chicken and peas to the saucepan and simmer for 5 minutes or until heated through.

STEP 5


Taste and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper. Serve each bowl topped with a dollop of almond and parsley pesto.

Ingredients

  • 2 € zest of 1 lemon

  • 2 € 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 2 € 1 onion, peeled, halved
  • 2 € 2 sticks celery, halved
  • 2 € 3 carrots (about 400g), peeled, diced 1cm
  • 2 € 1 cup frozen peas, thawed
