STEP 1


Heat 1‚ÅÑ2cm grapeseed oil in a frying pan until hot but not smoking then carefully add the eggplant cubes and fry evenly on all sides

STEP 2


Continue frying for 5-10 minutes until the eggplant is well browned and tender, then remove from the pan with a slotted spoon and put in a colander to drain. Season with salt and freshly ground black pepper

STEP 3


Combine the honey and vinegar and mix with the eggplant while it is still warm.

STEP 4


With fresh oil in the pan, fry the garlic and cumin seeds until they are popping and coloured. Add the chickpeas, sultanas and paprika and continue frying until the chickpeas are coated and have some colour. Remove from the heat and mix with the eggplant.

STEP 5


Increase the heat and in the same pan, with no extra oil, fry the haloumi slices until they are brown and bubbling. Top the eggplant and chickpea salad with the haloumi and drizzle with the green olive and parsley sauce.
