SQUID

Squid & new potatoes with fried chorizo dressing

2 comment(s)

STEP 1


Put the extra virgin olive oil, vinegar, spring onions, olives, garlic, capers, tomatoes and coriander in a bowl and mix well. This is the dressing

STEP 2


Heat the olive oil in a frying pan then fry the chorizo for 4-5 minutes or until crisp but not dry. Add to the dressing and mix well. Taste and season with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

STEP 3


Put the potatoes in a saucepan of salted water. Bring to the boil and boil until tender. Drain well then crush slightly with a fork and arrange on a warmed serving platter.

STEP 4


Meanwhile, heat a ridged grill or heavy frying pan until very hot. Season the squid pieces then fry, in batches, on the dry grill or frying pan until they colour and curl up. Put the squid on the potatoes then spoon the chorizo dressing over the top and serve immediately.

Ingredients

  • 2 € 5 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 € 2 tablespoons sherry vinegar
  • 2 € 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 € 1⁄2 cup pitted black olives
  • 2 € 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 2 € 2 tablespoons capers
  • 2 € 250g cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 2 € 2 tablespoons chopped coriander
  • 2 € 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 € 1 spicy chorizo, diced 1cm
  • 2 € 900g small potatoes, well scrubbed
  • 2 € 6 (about 600g) squid tubes, sliced down one side, opened out flat, scored with a dinner knife in a criss-cross pattern on the inside, tubes cut into quarters
