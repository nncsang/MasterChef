FONDUE

Fondue du fromage

2 comment(s)

STEP 1


Whatever your taste, go for a quality product that will make less fat and give a more flavorful fondue. Here we have chosen to use the County of Beaufort and Emmental cheese in equal proportion.

STEP 2


In a pot, pour about a dry white wine when it starts to boil, add the cheese cut into thin slices (you can also grate).

STEP 3


When the cheese begins to melt, stir to make molten homogeneous. Dissolve half a teaspoon of cornstarch (cornflour kind) in a kirsch bottom and pour in the melted to give it a little more consistency and flavor.
